# **App Name**: GearLink CRM

## Core Features:

- Inventory Display: Show a visual of total stocks and parts with names.
- Customer Management: Record and update customer contact information and purchase history.
- Product Listing: Maintain an organized, searchable catalog of auto parts and accessories.
- Sales Report: Generate a visual representation of how many products are sold in which time frame.
- Invoice Generation: Create and send invoices and manage payment records.
- Smart Part Finder: A tool that recommends parts and accessories that fit the make, model, and year of the user's vehicle.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, vibrant blue (#3F51B5) to inspire trust and efficiency.
- Background color: Light, desaturated blue (#E8EAF6).
- Accent color: Muted teal (#4DB6AC), drawing the eye without overwhelming.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif providing a modern look.
- Use clean, simple icons to represent different product categories and CRM functions.
- Maintain a clear and consistent layout throughout the CRM interface for easy navigation.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience during actions like adding or updating data.
